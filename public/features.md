# Capta Features — Complete List

Capta is an AI-powered front office for home service contractors. Maria, the AI receptionist, answers every call 24/7 in English and Spanish — booking appointments, generating estimates, recovering missed calls, and managing follow-ups automatically.

## Revenue Recovery (6 Features)

**Missed Call Recovery SMS**
When a caller hangs up, Maria auto-texts them within 60 seconds to bring them back. Recovers jobs that would otherwise go to competitors.

**AI Job Intake**
Maria asks the right questions — problem type, property address, urgency, photos — and creates a complete job card automatically. No manual data entry.

**AI Estimate Generation**
Based on job details and your pricing rules, Maria generates a price range on the call and texts it to the customer for review.

**Job Cards with Photo Intake**
Callers text photos of the job site. Images attach to the job card alongside all details, giving you full context before arriving.

**Owner Response Loop**
You get a text with the job summary and estimate. Approve, adjust, or decline with one tap — the customer is notified instantly.

**Estimate Follow-Up Automation**
Cold estimates get automatic follow-ups on a schedule you set. Maria re-engages leads that haven't responded, keeping your pipeline warm without manual effort.

## Calls & Voice (6 Features)

**24/7 Bilingual Answering**
Every call answered in English or Spanish. Auto-detects the caller's language — no phone menus, no press-1-for-English. Native bilingual, not translation software.

**Returning Caller Recognition**
Recognizes repeat callers automatically. Greets them by context and picks up where the last conversation left off.

**Emergency Detection + Live Transfer**
Detects emergency keywords like "gas leak" or "flooding" and immediately transfers the call to your emergency number. Sends you an SMS alert.

**After-Hours Intelligent Routing**
Different behavior for business hours vs. nights and weekends. Takes messages, books next-day appointments, or transfers urgencies.

**Custom Greetings & Personality**
Name her, choose her personality (friendly, professional, warm), set preferred phrases, and define off-limits topics.

**Recordings + Transcripts**
Full audio recording and AI-generated transcript for every call. Searchable, filterable, and exportable from your dashboard.

## Scheduling (4 Features)

**Appointment Management**
Maria books, reschedules, and cancels appointments through natural conversation. Your schedule stays organized without manual entry.

**Voice Booking**
Callers book appointments naturally through conversation — no app downloads, no online forms, no hold music.

**SMS Confirmations**
Instant text confirmation sent to the caller with appointment details, your business info, and the service address.

**Appointment Reminders**
Automated outbound reminders reduce no-shows. Sent via SMS at configurable intervals before the appointment.

## Growth & Automation (5 Features)

**Customer Recall**
Automatically re-engage past customers for seasonal maintenance, annual inspections, or follow-up work. Turns one-time jobs into recurring revenue.

**Google Review Requests**
After a completed job, Maria texts the customer asking for a Google review. Builds your online reputation on autopilot.

**Weekly Digest**
Comprehensive weekly summary — calls, appointments, estimates, revenue recovered, and key metrics delivered to your inbox.

**Partner Referral Network**
Get referrals from other trades when they can't handle a job. A plumber refers HVAC leads, an electrician refers plumbing leads.

**Outbound Call Automation**
Maria makes outbound calls for appointment confirmations, follow-ups, and customer re-engagement — not just inbound.

## Business Tools (4 Features)

**Auto-Populated CRM**
Every caller becomes a customer record automatically. Phone, name, call history, appointments, and notes — all in one place. Zero data entry.

**Estimate Pipeline**
Track every estimate from request to signed. See status, follow-up history, and close rates across all your jobs.

**AI-Powered Call Summaries**
AI reads the transcript and generates a concise summary with action items. Know what happened without listening to the call.

**CSV Import**
Import your existing customer database from any CRM or spreadsheet. Capta maps the fields and gives Maria full context from day one.

Real-time metrics: calls answered, appointments booked, revenue recovered, response times, and trends over time.

## Compliance (3 Features)

**GDPR/CCPA/TCPA Compliant**
Capta handles the legal stuff — GDPR, CCPA, and TCPA compliance built in so you don't have to think about it.

## Trade-Specific Examples

**Plumbing:** "I've got water leaking from under the kitchen sink." Maria collects address, asks for photos, generates $250–$400 estimate for supply line repair, texts to owner for one-tap approval.

**HVAC:** "My AC stopped cooling and it's 95 degrees." Maria flags urgency, collects unit age/model, generates diagnostic visit estimate, books next available slot.

**Electrical:** "Half my outlets stopped working after the storm." Maria asks about panel/breaker status, generates estimate for circuit troubleshoot, sends photos from caller.

**Roofing:** "I've got a leak in my attic after the rain." Maria collects roof age, damage area, insurance info, generates inspection estimate, schedules visit — all in the same call.
